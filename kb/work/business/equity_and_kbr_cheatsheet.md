# Equity & KBR (Kontrollbalansräkning) — Cheatsheet

**Threshold:** If **equity < 50%** of registered share capital (Persson Tech AB: 25,000 SEK → threshold 12,500 SEK), the **board must immediately** prepare a **Control Balance Sheet (Kontrollbalansräkning, KBR)** and convene a shareholders’ meeting per ABL.  
- Practical rule: keep an eye on accumulated losses; a single 200 USD SaaS won’t matter alone, but early large costs can.  
- If the KBR confirms <50%, call the first control meeting; there are formal steps to restore equity within the statutory window.
