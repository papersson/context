# Compliance Calendar — Persson Tech AB

> Keep this short; check “Viktiga datum” for your exact schedule.

## Monthly
- **VAT return (momsdeklaration):** File **by the 12th of the second month after the period ends.**  
  Examples: Oct → due Dec 12; Nov → due Jan 12.  
  Check exact dates under “Viktiga datum.”
- **Preliminary corporate tax (debiterad F-skatt):** Pay so it lands by the **12th** each month. Next shown in your skattekonto: **9 613 SEK due 2025-11-12**.


## Annual
- **AGM (årsstämma):** within **6 months** after year-end.  
- **Annual report to Bolagsverket (årsredovisning):** **within 7 months** after year-end.  
  https://bolagsverket.se/foretag/aktiebolag/arsredovisningforaktiebolag.759.html  
  https://bolagsverket.se/foretag/aktiebolag/arsredovisningforaktiebolag/taframenarsredovisning.793.html
