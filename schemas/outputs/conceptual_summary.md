# Output Schema: conceptual_summary

Defines the structure produced by `prompts/synthesis/conceptual_summary.md`.

## Content
- Direct exposition of how the system/idea works
- No meta-framing (no mentions of authors, sources, or “the paper argues”)
- Active voice, present tense
- Make abstract concepts concrete via mechanisms and dynamics

## Structure
- Plain paragraphs; no headings required
- Natural logical flow so insights cascade into each other
